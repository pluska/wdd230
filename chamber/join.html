Membership Levels

No Profit Membership

Cost: No cost

This introductory membership level provides essential benefits for individuals or organizations dedicated to economic progress:

  • Access to all events
  • Regular newsletters and economic insights
  • Networking opportunities with industry leaders
  • Priority access to selected workshops
  • Exclusive access to our online resource center
  • Discounted rates for specialized conferences
  • Complimentary consultation session with our economic experts

Bronze Membership

Cost: $50/month

This membership tier offers enhanced benefits for those seeking a deeper engagement with economic matters:

  • All benefits of the No Profit Membership
  • Access to premium workshops and seminars
  • Invitations to exclusive industry events
  • Customized economic reports and analysis
  • Priority registration for conferences and symposiums
  • Extended consultation sessions with our economic experts

Silver Membership

Cost: $100/month

For individuals and organizations looking for even greater involvement in economic discussions and strategies:

  • All benefits of the Bronze Membership
  • Access to specialized roundtable discussions
  • Exclusive access to pre-release economic reports
  • Personalized economic forecasting and trend analysis
  • Invitation to an annual private economic summit
  • Discounted rates for personalized economic workshops
  • Premium consultation sessions with our top economic experts

Gold Membership

Cost: $200/month

Our most comprehensive membership level, tailored for individuals and organizations committed to driving economic growth:

  • All benefits of the Silver Membership
  • Exclusive access to closed-door economic forums
  • Customized economic research projects
  • Priority access to international economic delegations
  • Personalized economic policy advocacy
  • Complimentary access to our flagship conferences
  • Unlimited premium consultation sessions with our distinguished economic experts
