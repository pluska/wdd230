Visual Hierarchy

Netflix

Netflix Website

In Netflix's design, you can see that the brand in the navigation bar stands out in size along with "Unlimited movies, TV shows, and more" and the registration. This allows the user to focus on recognizing the brand, its value proposition and the result they desire.

Rule of Thirds

Squarespace

Squarespace Website

Squarespace uses the rule of thirds in its design to divide the text from the images in order to focus the user's attention in an orderly fashion to what is important, which in your case would be the messages about your value proposition.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple Website

In Apple's design, white space and simplicity are essential. The balanced layout and white background highlight the products, creating a clean and spacious experience. Legible typography and logical organization make navigation easy. Key elements, such as the logo and product names, are presented prominently for easy identification.
